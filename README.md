## 🏢 Business Structures in India

| Structure | Best For | Liability | Min People | Annual Filing |
|-----------|----------|-----------|------------|---------------|
| **Sole Proprietorship** | Freelancers, small shops | Unlimited | 1 | ITR only |
| **Partnership** | Small businesses, CA firms | Unlimited | 2+ | ITR + Partnership deed |
| **LLP** | Professional services, consultants | Limited | 2+ | MCA filing + ITR |
| **Private Limited** | Startups seeking funding | Limited | 2+ | ROC filing + Audit + ITR |
| **One Person Company** | Solo founders wanting limited liability | Limited | 1 | ROC filing + ITR |

### Which One to Choose?

```
Freelancing / Side hustle     → Sole Proprietorship
Small local business          → Partnership or LLP
Planning to raise funding     → Private Limited Company
Solo founder, limited budget  → OPC (One Person Company)
Large scale from Day 1        → Private Limited
```

## 📝 How to Register

### Private Limited Company (Most Popular for Startups)

| Step | Action | Time | Cost (approx) |
|------|--------|------|----------------|
| 1 | Get DSC (Digital Signature Certificate) | 1 day | Rs.1,500 |
| 2 | Get DIN (Director Identification Number) | 1-2 days | Rs.500 |
| 3 | Name Approval (SPICe+ form) | 2-3 days | Rs.1,000 |
| 4 | File SPICe+ (incorporation) | 3-5 days | Rs.7,000-15,000 |
| 5 | Get Certificate of Incorporation | Same day as approval | Included |
| 6 | Open Current Account | 1-2 days | Free |
| 7 | GST Registration (if turnover > Rs.20L) | 3-5 days | Free |

**Total time: 7-15 days | Total cost: Rs.10,000-25,000** (including CA fees)

### Documents Needed
```
- PAN Card of all directors
- Aadhaar Card
- Passport-size photos
- Address proof of registered office
- Electricity bill / Rent agreement
- NOC from property owner
- Bank statement (6 months)
```

## 🇮🇳 Startup India Scheme Benefits

Register at: **startupindia.gov.in**

| Benefit | Details |
|---------|---------|
| **Tax Holiday** | 3 years income tax exemption (out of first 10 years) |
| **Angel Tax Exemption** | No tax on investments received from angels |
| **Self-Certification** | 6 labour laws + 3 environmental laws (no inspector visits) |
| **Fast Patent Filing** | 80% rebate on patent filing fees |
| **Easy Winding Up** | Close company in 90 days (vs 180 days normal) |
| **Government Tenders** | Can bid without prior experience/turnover |
| **Fund of Funds** | Access to Rs.10,000 Cr government fund (via VCs) |

### Eligibility
```
✅ Company/LLP/Partnership incorporated in India
✅ Less than 10 years from incorporation
✅ Annual turnover < Rs.100 Crore
✅ Working towards innovation/improvement
✅ Not formed by splitting an existing business
```

## 💰 Funding Your Startup

### Funding Stages

```
Idea Stage:     Bootstrapping / Friends & Family (Rs.1-10L)
        ↓
MVP Built:      Angel Investors / Incubators (Rs.10-50L)
        ↓
Early Traction: Seed Round / Angel Networks (Rs.50L-3Cr)
        ↓
Growing:        Series A / VCs (Rs.3-30Cr)
        ↓
Scaling:        Series B, C... / Large VCs (Rs.30Cr+)
```

### Funding Sources in India

| Source | Amount | What They Want |
|--------|--------|----------------|
| **Bootstrapping** | Your savings | Nothing — you own 100% |
| **Family & Friends** | Rs.1-10L | Trust in you |
| **Angel Investors** | Rs.5-50L | Equity (10-25%), strong team |
| **Incubators** | Rs.5-25L + mentoring | Small equity (5-10%) |
| **Shark Tank India** | Rs.10L-2Cr | Equity + good story + traction |
| **Venture Capital** | Rs.1Cr+ | High growth potential, 20-40% equity |
| **Bank Loan (Mudra)** | Rs.10L max | Collateral-free, repayment plan |
| **Government Grants** | Varies | Application + eligibility |

### Indian Angel Networks & VCs
```
Angels: Indian Angel Network, LetsVenture, AngelList India, Mumbai Angels
VCs: Sequoia India (Peak XV), Accel, Elevation, Matrix, Blume Ventures
Incubators: T-Hub, IIM Bangalore NSRCEL, IIT Bombay (SINE), NASSCOM 10K
```

## ✅ Compliance Checklist

### Monthly
- [ ] GST Return filing (GSTR-1, GSTR-3B) by 11th & 20th
- [ ] TDS deposit by 7th (if applicable)
- [ ] Salary processing & PF deposit by 15th

### Quarterly
- [ ] TDS Return (24Q, 26Q)
- [ ] Advance Tax (if liability > Rs.10,000)

### Annually
- [ ] Income Tax Return
- [ ] Company Annual Return (MGT-7) 
- [ ] Financial Statement filing (AOC-4)
- [ ] Statutory Audit (if Pvt Ltd)
- [ ] GST Annual Return (GSTR-9)
- [ ] Director KYC update

## 💸 Realistic Costs to Start

### Minimum Viable Budget

| Item | Solo/Bootstrap | With Small Team |
|------|----------------|-----------------|
| Company Registration | Rs.15,000 | Rs.15,000 |
| Domain + Hosting | Rs.5,000/yr | Rs.5,000/yr |
| Basic Tools (email, etc) | Rs.5,000/yr | Rs.15,000/yr |
| CA/Compliance | Rs.20,000/yr | Rs.50,000/yr |
| Co-working Space | Rs.5,000/mo | Rs.15,000/mo |
| Marketing (initial) | Rs.10,000 | Rs.50,000 |
| **Total Year 1** | **~Rs.1.5-2L** | **~Rs.5-8L** |

### What You DON'T Need to Start
```
❌ Fancy office (work from home/co-working)
❌ Large team (start solo or with 1 co-founder)
❌ Perfect product (launch MVP, iterate)
❌ MBA degree (execution > qualification)
❌ Lakhs in savings (validate idea with Rs.10K)
```

## 🦈 Lessons from Shark Tank India

### What Sharks Look For

| Factor | What Impresses Them |
|--------|---------------------|
| **Revenue** | Actual sales > just an idea |
| **Unit Economics** | Positive margins per order |
| **Moat** | What stops others from copying you? |
| **Team** | Why are YOU the right person? |
| **Scalability** | Can this be 100x? |
| **Market Size** | Large addressable market in India |

### Common Reasons for Rejection
```
❌ "Your valuation is too high" (Asking Rs.5Cr for Rs.10L revenue)
❌ "There's no moat" (Anyone can copy this tomorrow)
❌ "Unit economics don't work" (Losing money on every sale)
❌ "It's a feature, not a product" (Too small to be a business)
❌ "You don't know your numbers" (Can't answer basic questions)
```

### Valuation Rules of Thumb
```
Pre-revenue idea:     Rs.1-3 Cr (max)
Early revenue:        3-5x annual revenue
Growing startup:      8-15x annual revenue
Profitable + growing: 15-30x net profit
```

## 📊 Key Startup Metrics

| Metric | What it Measures | Formula |
|--------|------------------|---------|
| **CAC** | Cost to Acquire 1 Customer | Total Marketing Spend / New Customers |
| **LTV** | Lifetime Value of 1 Customer | Avg Order Value × Purchase Frequency × Retention |
| **Burn Rate** | Money spent per month | Monthly Expenses - Monthly Revenue |
| **Runway** | Months before money runs out | Cash in Bank / Monthly Burn Rate |
| **MRR** | Monthly Recurring Revenue | Subscribers × Monthly Price |
| **Churn Rate** | Customers leaving per month | Lost Customers / Total Customers × 100 |
| **GMV** | Gross Merchandise Value | Total value of transactions |

### Healthy Startup Rule
```
LTV > 3x CAC (you're profitable per customer)
Burn Rate < Revenue (path to profitability)
Runway > 12 months (you have time to figure things out)
MRR growing > 15% month-on-month (strong growth)
```
