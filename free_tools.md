## Website
- WordPress (free) / Framer

- Google Workspace (email)

## Finance
- Zoho Books (free up to Rs.30L revenue)

- ClearTax (GST filing)

## Marketing
- Canva (social media graphics)
- Buffer (scheduling posts)
- Google My Business (local SEO)

## Legal

- Cleartax (registration)

## Communication
- Slack (free tier)
- Google Meet
